One Narrative Thread for Your Book
The most compelling single story arc:

In October 2020, the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) sent GitHub a legal notice demanding removal of youtube-dl — a free tool used by journalists, archivists, researchers, and millions of ordinary people. GitHub complied within hours. Within days, the entire developer community erupted. The Electronic Frontier Foundation sent a formal legal rebuttal. GitHub's own CEO publicly expressed discomfort with the decision. One week later, youtube-dl was restored — and the incident became the most discussed example of platform power, DMCA overreach, and open source rights in the modern internet era.

That single event touches copyright law, platform power, open source culture, developer rights, and digital ownership all at once — which sounds like exactly the kind of material your book needs.
